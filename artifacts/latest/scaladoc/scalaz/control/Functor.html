trait Functor[F]
extends AnyRef
Covariant function application in an environment. i.e. a covariant Functor.

All functor instances must satisfy 2 laws:

  1. identity
    forall a. a == fmap(identity, a)
  2. composition
    forall a f g. fmap(f compose g, a) == fmap(f, fmap(g, a))
