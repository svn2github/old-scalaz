trait Monoid[M]
extends Zero[M] with Semigroup[M]
A categorical monoid.

All monoid instances must satisfy the semigroup law and 2 additional laws:

  1. left identity
    forall a. append(zero, a) == a
  2. right identity
    forall a. append(a, zero) == a
