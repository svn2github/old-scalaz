trait MonadPlus[M]
extends Monad[M] with Plus[M]
A monad with plus.

All monad-plus instances must satisfy the monad laws and 1 additional law:

  1. associativity
    forall a b c. plus(a, plus(a, b)) == plus(plus(a, b), c)
