Class Summary
sealed trait OptionValidator [-A, B] extends AnyRef
Validators using a kleisli structure over scala.Option.
sealed trait Validation [+E, +A] extends AnyRef
A wrapper around scala.Either that has renamed identifiers (e.g. Left -> Fail, Right -> Success) for validation. Errors are stored on the Left side of the underlying Either. This type also has a different Applicative instance to Either in that errors are accumulated on the left (should one or more be encountered).
