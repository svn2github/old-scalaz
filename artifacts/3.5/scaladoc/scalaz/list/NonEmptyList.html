trait NonEmptyList
[source: scalaz/list/NonEmptyList.scala]
sealed trait NonEmptyList[+A]
extends AnyRef
A List that has at least one element and therefore, a total head and tail. The structure is backed by a scala.List and so is fully evaluated.
