scalaz.validation

trait Validation
[source: scalaz/validation/Validation.scala]
sealed trait Validation[E, A]
extends AnyRef
A wrapper around scala.Either that has renamed identifiers (e.g. Left -> Fail, Right -> Success) for validation. Errors are typically stored on the Left side of the underlying Either. This type also has a different Applicative instance to Either in that errors are accumulated on the left (should one or more be encountered).
